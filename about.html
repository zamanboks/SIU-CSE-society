about cse society

The goal of the CSE Society is to introduce and excite students to the wide range of possibilities in Computer Science so that they can prepare themselves and cope with the most advanced technology in the world. The Society provides a strong platform for the students to practically implement their innovative ideas in computer for different applications.



CSE Society is a non-political departmental organization. In Sylhet International University Bangladesh, CSE society is the largest organization consists of all students and teachers of CSE department. The objective of the CSE Society is to promote Computer Science & Engineering awareness among the students in the CSE by organizing technical activities such as lectures, workshop, quizzes and excursion and to foster relationship among the past and present students and the teachers of the CSE by organizing various departmental events such as annual day celebration, fresher's welcome, farewell etc.
